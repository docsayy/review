Gliomas are the most common primary brain tumors.

Astrocytoma is a tumor of astrocytes.

Glioblastoma is the most aggressive astrocytoma.

Glioblastoma shows necrosis and microvascular proliferation.

Oligodendroglioma is associated with 1p/19q co-deletion.

Meningioma arises from arachnoid cells.

Meningioma is usually benign and slow-growing.

Medulloblastoma is a malignant pediatric cerebellar tumor.

Primary CNS lymphoma is associated with EBV in immunocompromised patients.
