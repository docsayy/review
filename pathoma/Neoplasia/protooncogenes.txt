Cancer results from mutation of proto-oncogenes.

Cancer also results from loss of tumor suppressor genes.

Cancer also results from dysregulation of apoptosis.

Proto-oncogenes regulate normal cell growth.

Mutated proto-oncogenes become oncogenes.

PDGFB overexpression causes astrocytoma.

ERBB2 amplification causes breast carcinoma.

RET mutation causes MEN2 and medullary thyroid carcinoma.

KIT mutation causes gastrointestinal stromal tumors.

RAS mutation occurs in many carcinomas and melanomas.

ABL translocation t(9;22) causes CML.

C-MYC translocation t(8;14) causes Burkitt lymphoma.

N-MYC amplification causes neuroblastoma.

Cyclin D1 translocation t(11;14) causes mantle cell lymphoma.
