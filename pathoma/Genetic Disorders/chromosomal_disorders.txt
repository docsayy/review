Aneuploidy is abnormal chromosome number.

Nondisjunction causes aneuploidy.

Trisomy 21 causes Down syndrome.

Down syndrome presents with intellectual disability and characteristic facial features.

Trisomy 18 causes Edwards syndrome.

Trisomy 13 causes Patau syndrome.

Turner syndrome is monosomy X.

Klinefelter syndrome is XXY.

Microdeletions cause loss of small chromosomal segments.

22q11 deletion causes DiGeorge syndrome.
