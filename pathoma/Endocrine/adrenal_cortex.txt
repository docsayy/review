The adrenal cortex produces mineralocorticoids, glucocorticoids, and androgens.

Cushing syndrome is excess cortisol production.

Cushing disease is caused by pituitary ACTH overproduction.

Adrenal adenoma can cause Cushing syndrome.

Addison disease is primary adrenal insufficiency.

Addison disease causes hypotension, hyperkalemia, and hyperpigmentation.

Congenital adrenal hyperplasia is enzyme deficiency in cortisol synthesis.

21-hydroxylase deficiency is the most common CAH.
