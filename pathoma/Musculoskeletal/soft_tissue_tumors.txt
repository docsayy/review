Soft tissue tumors arise from mesenchymal tissues.

Lipoma is a benign tumor of adipose tissue.

Liposarcoma is a malignant tumor of adipose tissue.

Leiomyoma is a benign smooth muscle tumor.

Leiomyosarcoma is a malignant smooth muscle tumor.

Rhabdomyoma is a benign skeletal muscle tumor.

Rhabdomyosarcoma is a malignant skeletal muscle tumor.

Synovial sarcoma is a malignant soft tissue tumor near joints.

Kaposi sarcoma is a vascular tumor associated with HHV-8 infection.
