Osteosarcoma is a malignant bone tumor producing osteoid.

Osteosarcoma commonly arises in the metaphysis of long bones.

Osteosarcoma is associated with RB gene mutation.

Ewing sarcoma is a malignant bone tumor of small round blue cells.

Ewing sarcoma is associated with t(11;22) translocation.

Chondrosarcoma is a malignant cartilage-forming tumor.

Giant cell tumor of bone contains multinucleated osteoclast-like cells.

Metastatic carcinoma is the most common malignant tumor in bone.

Prostate cancer commonly causes blastic bone metastases.

Breast cancer commonly causes lytic bone metastases.
