Osteoporosis is decreased bone mass with normal mineralization.

Osteoporosis increases fracture risk.

Postmenopausal estrogen loss is a major cause of osteoporosis.

Chronic steroid use causes osteoporosis.

Osteomalacia is defective bone mineralization in adults.

Rickets is defective bone mineralization in children.

Vitamin D deficiency causes osteomalacia and rickets.

Hyperparathyroidism causes increased bone resorption.

Paget disease causes disorganized bone remodeling.

Paget disease produces enlarged but weak bones.
