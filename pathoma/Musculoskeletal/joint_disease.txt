Osteoarthritis is degeneration of articular cartilage.

Osteoarthritis causes joint space narrowing and osteophytes.

Rheumatoid arthritis is autoimmune inflammatory joint disease.

Rheumatoid arthritis causes synovial pannus formation.

Rheumatoid arthritis can cause joint destruction and deformity.

Gout is caused by monosodium urate crystal deposition.

Pseudogout is caused by calcium pyrophosphate crystal deposition.
