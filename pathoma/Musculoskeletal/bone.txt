Bone is composed of cortical bone and trabecular bone.

Osteoblasts form new bone matrix.

Osteoclasts resorb bone.

Osteocytes maintain bone.

Type I collagen is the main organic component of bone.

Hydroxyapatite is the main inorganic component of bone.

Bone remodeling balances osteoblast and osteoclast activity.

Parathyroid hormone increases osteoclast activity.

Vitamin D promotes calcium absorption and bone mineralization.
