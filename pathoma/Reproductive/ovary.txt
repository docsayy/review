The ovary contains follicles at various stages of development.

Follicular cysts result from failure of follicle rupture.

Corpus luteum cysts form after ovulation.

Polycystic ovary syndrome is characterized by multiple ovarian cysts and hyperandrogenism.

Polycystic ovary syndrome causes irregular menses and infertility.

Ovarian serous carcinoma is the most common ovarian malignancy.

Serous carcinoma is associated with BRCA mutations.

Ovarian cancer often presents late with abdominal distension.

Ascites is a common finding in ovarian carcinoma.
