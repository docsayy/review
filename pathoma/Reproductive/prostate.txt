The prostate surrounds the prostatic urethra.

Benign prostatic hyperplasia causes enlargement of the prostate.

BPH compresses the urethra and causes urinary obstruction.

BPH arises from the transitional zone of the prostate.

Prostate adenocarcinoma is the most common cancer in men.

Prostate cancer arises from the peripheral zone of the prostate.

Prostate cancer may be detected by elevated PSA.

Gleason grading is used to assess prostate cancer aggressiveness.

Prostate cancer commonly metastasizes to bone.
