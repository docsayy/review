The breast contains lobules and ducts.

Fibrocystic change is a benign breast condition.

Fibrocystic change causes cyst formation and fibrosis.

Fibroadenoma is a benign breast tumor in young women.

Ductal carcinoma in situ is malignant cells confined to ducts.

Invasive ductal carcinoma is the most common breast cancer.

Invasive lobular carcinoma lacks E-cadherin expression.

Breast cancer may metastasize to bone.

BRCA1 and BRCA2 mutations increase breast cancer risk.
