The endometrium undergoes cyclic hormonal changes.

Endometrial hyperplasia results from unopposed estrogen stimulation.

Endometrial hyperplasia increases risk of endometrial carcinoma.

Endometrial carcinoma commonly presents with postmenopausal bleeding.

Leiomyoma is a benign smooth muscle tumor of the uterus.

Leiomyoma is also called a uterine fibroid.

Leiomyomas may cause heavy menstrual bleeding.

Leiomyosarcoma is a malignant smooth muscle tumor.
