The testes contain seminiferous tubules for sperm production.

Sertoli cells support spermatogenesis.

Leydig cells produce testosterone.

Cryptorchidism is failure of testicular descent.

Cryptorchidism increases risk of infertility and testicular cancer.

Testicular torsion causes acute ischemic injury to the testis.

Testicular torsion presents with sudden severe scrotal pain.
