Myeloproliferative neoplasms cause overproduction of blood cells.

Polycythemia vera is caused by JAK2 mutation.

Polycythemia vera increases red cells, white cells, and platelets.

Essential thrombocythemia causes isolated platelet overproduction.

Primary myelofibrosis causes marrow fibrosis.

Myeloproliferative neoplasms may transform into acute leukemia.
