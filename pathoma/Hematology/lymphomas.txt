Lymphoma is malignancy of lymphoid tissue.

Hodgkin lymphoma contains Reed–Sternberg cells.

Reed–Sternberg cells are CD15 and CD30 positive.

Hodgkin lymphoma spreads in an orderly nodal pattern.

Non-Hodgkin lymphoma spreads in a non-orderly pattern.

Burkitt lymphoma is associated with t(8;14) MYC translocation.

Burkitt lymphoma shows a starry-sky pattern.

Follicular lymphoma is associated with t(14;18) BCL2 translocation.

Diffuse large B-cell lymphoma is the most common non-Hodgkin lymphoma.
