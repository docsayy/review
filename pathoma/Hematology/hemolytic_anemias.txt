Hemolytic anemia is premature destruction of red blood cells.

Extravascular hemolysis occurs in the spleen.

Intravascular hemolysis occurs within blood vessels.

Hereditary spherocytosis is caused by spectrin deficiency.

Hereditary spherocytosis causes splenomegaly and spherocytes.

G6PD deficiency causes oxidative hemolysis.

G6PD deficiency is X-linked recessive.

Sickle cell disease is caused by hemoglobin S.

Hemoglobin S polymerizes under low oxygen conditions.

Sickle cell disease causes vaso-occlusion and pain crises.

Autoimmune hemolytic anemia is caused by antibodies against red cells.

Cold agglutinin disease is mediated by IgM antibodies.
