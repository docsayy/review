Macrocytic anemia is caused by impaired DNA synthesis.

Vitamin B12 deficiency causes macrocytic anemia.

Vitamin B12 deficiency may result from loss of intrinsic factor.

Intrinsic factor is produced by gastric parietal cells.

Folate deficiency causes macrocytic anemia.

Folate deficiency occurs in malnutrition and alcoholism.

Macrocytic anemia shows hypersegmented neutrophils.
