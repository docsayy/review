Acute leukemia is malignancy of hematopoietic blasts.

Acute leukemia causes bone marrow failure.

Acute myelogenous leukemia contains Auer rods.

Auer rods are myeloperoxidase-positive inclusions.

Acute promyelocytic leukemia is caused by t(15;17).

Acute promyelocytic leukemia responds to all-trans retinoic acid.

Acute lymphoblastic leukemia is the most common childhood leukemia.

TdT is positive in lymphoblastic leukemia.
