Amyloid is misfolded protein deposited extracellularly.

Amyloid has beta-pleated sheet structure.

Amyloid stains Congo red and shows apple-green birefringence.

Primary amyloidosis is light chain deposition.

Secondary amyloidosis is serum amyloid A deposition.

Kidney involvement causes nephrotic syndrome in amyloidosis.

Familial Mediterranean fever causes secondary amyloidosis.

Senile cardiac amyloidosis is transthyretin deposition.

Alzheimer disease involves beta-amyloid plaques.

Dialysis can cause beta-2 microglobulin amyloid in joints.

Medullary thyroid carcinoma causes calcitonin amyloid.
