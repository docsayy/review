Free radicals are molecules with unpaired outer electrons.

Hydroxyl radical is the most damaging free radical.

Ionizing radiation generates hydroxyl radicals.

NADPH oxidase in neutrophils generates superoxide radicals.

Free radicals damage DNA, proteins, and lipids.

Antioxidants neutralize free radicals.

Superoxide dismutase removes superoxide radicals.

Catalase removes hydrogen peroxide.

Glutathione peroxidase removes hydroxyl radicals.

Carbon tetrachloride toxicity causes free radical liver injury.

Reperfusion injury increases free radical damage.
