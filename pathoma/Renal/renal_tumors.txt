Renal cell carcinoma arises from renal tubular epithelium.

Clear cell carcinoma is the most common renal cell carcinoma.

Renal cell carcinoma is associated with smoking and obesity.

Renal cell carcinoma may cause hematuria and flank pain.

Renal cell carcinoma can cause paraneoplastic syndromes.

Wilms tumor is a pediatric kidney tumor.

Wilms tumor is associated with WAGR syndrome.
