Basal cell carcinoma is the most common skin cancer.

Basal cell carcinoma rarely metastasizes.

Squamous cell carcinoma of skin is associated with sun exposure.

Squamous cell carcinoma can metastasize.

Melanoma is a malignant tumor of melanocytes.

Melanoma is strongly associated with UV exposure.

ABCDE criteria are used to assess melanoma.

Kaposi sarcoma is a vascular tumor associated with HHV-8.
