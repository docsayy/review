Dermatitis is inflammation of the skin.

Eczema is a pruritic inflammatory skin condition.

Atopic dermatitis is associated with allergies and asthma.

Contact dermatitis results from exposure to irritants or allergens.

Psoriasis is a chronic inflammatory disease with scaly plaques.

Psoriasis is caused by increased keratinocyte proliferation.

Seborrheic dermatitis affects areas rich in sebaceous glands.
