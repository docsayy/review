The epidermis is composed of stratified squamous epithelium.

Keratinocytes are the primary cells of the epidermis.

Melanocytes produce melanin pigment.

Langerhans cells are antigen-presenting immune cells in the skin.

Merkel cells are mechanoreceptors in the epidermis.

The dermis contains connective tissue, blood vessels, and nerves.

The subcutaneous tissue contains adipose tissue.
