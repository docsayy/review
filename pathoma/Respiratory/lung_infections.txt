Pneumonia is infection of the lung parenchyma.

Lobar pneumonia affects an entire lobe.

Bronchopneumonia produces patchy lung involvement.

Aspiration pneumonia affects dependent lung segments.

Streptococcus pneumoniae is a common cause of lobar pneumonia.

Hospital-acquired pneumonia often involves gram-negative bacteria.

Viral pneumonia causes interstitial inflammation.

Mycoplasma pneumonia causes atypical pneumonia.
