Cholestasis is impaired bile flow.

Cholestasis causes jaundice and pruritus.

Gallstones form from cholesterol or pigment.

Cholesterol stones are associated with obesity and pregnancy.

Pigment stones are associated with hemolysis.

Cholecystitis is inflammation of the gallbladder.

Cholecystitis is usually caused by gallstone obstruction.

Ascending cholangitis is bacterial infection of bile ducts.

Ascending cholangitis presents with fever, jaundice, and right upper quadrant pain.
