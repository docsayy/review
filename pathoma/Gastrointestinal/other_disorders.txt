Hemorrhoids are dilated venous plexuses in the anal canal.

Portal hypertension predisposes to hemorrhoids.

Anal fissures cause painful rectal bleeding.

Meckel diverticulum is a remnant of the vitelline duct.

Meckel diverticulum may contain ectopic gastric mucosa.

Intussusception is telescoping of bowel segments.

Intussusception causes obstruction and ischemia.
