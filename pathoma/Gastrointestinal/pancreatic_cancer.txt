Pancreatic adenocarcinoma arises from ductal epithelium.

Pancreatic cancer is strongly associated with smoking.

Pancreatic cancer commonly arises in the head of the pancreas.

Obstruction of the bile duct causes painless jaundice in pancreatic head tumors.

Pancreatic cancer invades surrounding structures early.

Weight loss is a common presentation of pancreatic cancer.
