Carcinoid tumors arise from neuroendocrine cells.

Carcinoid tumors commonly occur in the small intestine.

Carcinoid tumors may metastasize to the liver.

Carcinoid syndrome is caused by serotonin release.

Carcinoid syndrome causes flushing, diarrhea, and wheezing.

Carcinoid heart disease causes right-sided valvular fibrosis.
