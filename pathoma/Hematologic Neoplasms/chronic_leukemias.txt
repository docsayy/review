Chronic leukemia is a malignancy of mature hematopoietic cells.

Chronic myelogenous leukemia is caused by t(9;22) BCR-ABL.

BCR-ABL is a constitutively active tyrosine kinase.

Chronic myelogenous leukemia presents with splenomegaly and high WBC count.

Imatinib inhibits BCR-ABL tyrosine kinase.

Chronic lymphocytic leukemia is a malignancy of mature B cells.

Chronic lymphocytic leukemia shows smudge cells on smear.

CLL is associated with autoimmune hemolytic anemia.
