Paraneoplastic syndromes are systemic effects of cancer not caused by local tumor invasion.

Small cell lung carcinoma can cause SIADH.

Small cell lung carcinoma can cause Cushing syndrome.

Squamous cell carcinoma of lung can cause hypercalcemia.

Renal cell carcinoma can cause erythropoietin overproduction.

Hepatocellular carcinoma can cause hypoglycemia.
