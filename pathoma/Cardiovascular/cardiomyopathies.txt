Dilated cardiomyopathy causes ventricular dilation and systolic dysfunction.

Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy is caused by sarcomere gene mutations.

Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy causes asymmetric septal hypertrophy.

Restrictive cardiomyopathy limits ventricular filling.

Amyloidosis is a common cause of restrictive cardiomyopathy.
